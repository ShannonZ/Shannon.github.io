Significance & Application

NMR well-logging technology is used to reservoir evaluation and formation evaluation. NMR technology can acquire abundant formation information, such as flowing liquid content in a formation, porosity, permeability, oil saturation, aperture size and distribution in rock and so on. The advantages of NMR technology is high precision, simple calibration, avoiding influence of slop, acquiring many parameters in one well- logging
